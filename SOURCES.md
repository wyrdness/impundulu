## Cultural Sensitivity Note

When researching the impundulu:
- Consult with Zulu, Xhosa, and Pondo cultural authorities
- Recognize the serious nature of witchcraft beliefs
- Understand potential dangers of witchcraft accusations
- Respect traditional knowledge holders
- Approach with cultural humility and awareness
- Consider ethical implications of documentation

**Warning**: Witchcraft accusations can lead to violence. Research and documentation must be conducted with extreme care and cultural sensitivity.
